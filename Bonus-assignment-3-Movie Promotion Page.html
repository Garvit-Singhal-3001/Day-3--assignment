Storyline

In a quiet town where the night holds dark secrets, a young woman discovers a mysterious journal that echoes the future — and the past. As she dives deeper into its pages, her reality begins to blur with the unknown.

Midnight Echoes is a haunting tale of memory, fate, and identity. As the line between what's real and what's imagined begins to vanish, she must face the truth hidden in the echoes of the night.
